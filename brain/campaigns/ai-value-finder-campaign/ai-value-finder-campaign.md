# AI Value Finder Campaign

## About

A 3-day engagement with an enterprise's leadership team to map out the operations and workflows that need automation. The output is a board-ready AI roadmap built entirely from the company's own operational reality.

## Objective

Establish credibility with Fortune 500 decision-makers as the go-to partner for AI spend evaluation, converting that trust into demo bookings.

## Target ICP

CFOs, CEOs, decision-makers at fortune-500 companies, in tech, healthcare, finance and other domains

_None specified._
