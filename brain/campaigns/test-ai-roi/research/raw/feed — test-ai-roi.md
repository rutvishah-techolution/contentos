# Feed — Raw Research

## Summary

The AI industry is in a wild west phase right now, with massive spending but elusive ROI for most. Everyone's talking about agentic AI and sovereign AI, but a huge number of projects are stuck in 'pilot purgatory' or failing to deliver measurable value. The big story isn't just the tech, it's the cost explosion and the scramble to prove these investments actually pay off, especially as companies lay off staff to fund AI that often costs more than the people it replaced. Meanwhile, social media is getting flooded with AI-generated content, making authenticity a premium.

- **Source:** https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQFxzCPZDEtXExjzP0l2wApmxfkqsUjiou7aRu9zOApGumHdwn0mqe8SV_QNQhGp-ebDkmLm2ZlQbxOFvcw15QfqjE3cGmnyc6Yjo29OHxxlY_kqU-5xA3ZdvlCmj_G5cvTs-Godad0-5hg9Kg3KC3kXbE02r7rjcfT5bWPHhfLGV-c9Nhs3AI88VppxFiSqKRsdVyOvzkDh

The hidden costs of data transfer and storage are also eating into budgets, often making up more than half the total cost.

### 5. Agentic AI is a major trend for 2026, with enterprises shifting AI spend towards autonomous multi-step actions and expecting agentic projects to make up over 40% of their AI budgets.

- **Evidence:** AI Industry Landscape 2026 report
- **Source:** https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQHPPFykcPQ9dQjeqQ6M5JlxACZjMPb1frKdIxT6BZJ_L1vppCKgUixGRiS7SShYqH8RJrKrWTYuUnboQchmMZ_ZelJJ2U4knDq9LvEVNoWAyMI2VTqlJ2EBDRDPwKI=

But Gartner's warning that most early agentic projects will be canceled due to cost and governance issues is a huge red flag. Everyone's rushing in, but few are thinking about the real-world implications of autonomous systems making decisions.

### 6. Sovereign AI is expanding beyond governments to become a mainstream enterprise strategy, with large organizations wanting to own every critical layer of their AI stack.

- **Evidence:** Forbes, "The Enterprise AI Reckoning: The Next Phase Of The AI Economy"
- **Source:** https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQEctdc4osSHDJ7p_3OTvQjvY-YK_SHpGvZjCQ-s1uUbIhyOkT2QzOm5zGlZp40pIoufw_OP-YyrHg7JFTh8DxnARK38Y1QgtUHyioqBV6zVZUclgGiHyH0_W7TO0lfZtYXxQ9hA-3XsnnDIR9jnQlj8HMySGHT3bClnSyDT3UzqqyM1k3f2YuoZx-jwLA_yYrJh5KbDnvK8knp5pDB9W_iLm6ysx16RIKhK
- **Why it matters:** Forget just using someone else's model; companies are realizing they need to control their own data, models, and infrastructure for security, governance, and compliance. This isn't just about national policy anymore; it's about enterprise risk management and competitive advantage. Expect to see more companies building out their own internal AI factories.

### 7. AI is now a default part of social media marketing workflows, with 46% of marketers using AI for copy and content creation, but an authenticity backlash is reshaping what wins.

- **Evidence:** Blotato, "AI Social Media Trends 2026: What Creators Must Know"
- **Source:** https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQF0C4RUoZ7YDK5DNkQvioGgEp2Y_WfADitl3lyJNIQlC1VmmZTaKylSrvhU7nv0xs3vYQGq4shJ2gk5Zbvj_6fOyltMyy0YBX8cf-2wnJyLxf-xG24yp83XJ9bsS1Xc-l3Amo-rC8W8mpCfO7hJzrbOHmo=

The real play now is using AI for efficiency, then using humans for strategy and authenticity. Brands that just flood the zone with generic AI content are going to get ignored.
