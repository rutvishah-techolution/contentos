# Ai For Humans — Raw Research

## Summary

Okay, so here's the real talk: C-suite executives are *all in* on AI, but a huge chunk of their investments aren't delivering the goods. It's not because the tech is bad; it's because they're often missing a clear strategy for connecting AI to actual business outcomes and optimizing workflows *before* they automate. The struggle is real when it comes to proving ROI, integrating fragmented systems, and getting employees on board, but the companies that get it right are seeing massive efficiency gains and cost savings.
