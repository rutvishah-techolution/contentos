# Feed — Raw Research

## Summary

Everyone's talking about AI, but the C-suite is still struggling to prove its value. While adoption rates are high, a staggering majority of AI projects fail to deliver measurable ROI, often because companies are focused on activity instead of actual business outcomes. The real wins come from strategic implementation, clear governance, and a focus on workflows that translate directly into financial or operational gains, not just from throwing AI at every problem. If you're not mapping out how AI specifically solves a business problem and measuring that impact, you're just burning cash.
